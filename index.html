What is Energy Resilience?

⚡

Decentralized Solar

Generating power exactly where it is consumed reduces reliance on vulnerable, long-distance transmission lines prone to grid failure during extreme weather.

🔋

Smart Storage

Batteries store excess power created during sunny or windy periods, instantly kicking in to keep critical systems alive when the main grid goes completely dark.

🌐

Independent Microgrids

Localized grids can disconnect or "island" themselves from the primary grid during emergencies, ensuring hospitals, homes, and emergency hubs remain fully operational.
